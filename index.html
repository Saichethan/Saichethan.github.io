About me

I am a final year computer science undergraduate at Indian Institute of Information Technology, Bhagalpur. I am mostly interested in working on Multimodal Learning, NLP and their applications in real world. I am passionate about CS + X (Interdisciplinary) Research where X={Autonomous Systems, Social Systems}

At IIT Patna, I have worked with Dr. Sriparna Saha and Dr. Pushpak Bhattacharya in the AI-NLP-ML lab, where I explored different supervised and unsupervised learning algorithms for automatic summarization of scholarly articles. I also worked on the multimodal named entity recognition task on twitter data. I am currently exploring different sub domains of NLP.

I am interested in Multimodal Learning, because unlike formal languages natural languages don't have fixed set of rules(sarcasm, irony, emotion etc.,) in The 7% rule: fact, fiction, or misunderstanding author concluded 93% of understanding depends on non verbal cues, though 93% is debatable but from our real life experiece we also know non verbal cues gives us considerable amount of understanding, multimodal learning can leverage these non verbal cues for better understanding. My goal is to use machine learning to break language barriers and facilitate communication across languages and cultures

Apart from pursuing my academic interests, I am extremely passionate about upliftment of women (and other minorities) in technology. I lead the Google Developer Student Club at IIIT Bhagalpur, which works towards diversity and inclusion of minorities in tech. I am a huge advocate of diversity in CS Research, and always looking for opportunities to support them!
